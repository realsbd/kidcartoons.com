Utility

Not only will you be able to invest in an artist you believe in, but by owning a Cliff Hanger NFT, you will also receive the following utitlites.

1. 30% of the mint profits and 2% of the royalties on all future purchases will be put into a #community-fund to serve as giveaways/prizes, free merchandise, and any other investments that will benefit and strengthen Cliff Hanger NFT owners.

2. Owning a Cliff Hanger NFT gives you full IP rights to your specific Cliff Hanger NFT.

3. Owning a Cliff Hanger NFT provides owners with the opportunity to be placed on the whitelist for ALL of The Kid Toons NFT projects. Owners will be given access to a private channel in The Kid Tunes Discord server that will allow you to simply fill out a form to get placed on the whitelist. Everyone who owns a Cliff Hanger NFT will be given access to this channel, but it will be first come first serve as there are only a certain amount of whitelist spots for each project.

4. Owning a Cliff Hanger NFT provides owners with the opportunity to share their work/ideas to the “Spotlight” channel. Cliff Hanger NFT owners can pitch their art, music, business ideas, etc. to the Cliff Hanger team and we will pick our favorite entries to “Spotlight”.

The Spotlight channel will be visible to all members of The Kid Toons Discord server. The Kid Toons will spotlight people who are selected for 2 weeks - a Month or longer (depending on how many Cliff Hanger NFTs you own) at a time on the Discord server. This will serve as a great way to market and promote yourself/your company. You will be able to pitch as long as you own a Cliff Hanger NFT and will be given the opportunity to be able to be featured on the Spotlight channel multiple times.

5. Access to Virtual and In Person Networking events.

6. Once every Cliff Hanger NFT is minted, owners who minted a Cliff Hanger NFT will receive a signed print of their specific version of Cliff Hanger.
